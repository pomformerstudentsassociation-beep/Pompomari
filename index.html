Welcome to Pompomari Former Student Association
This Associationis exclusively for former Students(alumni) of pompomari. Our aim is unite past students,strengthen our bonds, support one another,and contribute positively to the growth and development of our alma mater and society at large. membership is strictly limited to individuals who attended and completed their studies at pompomari. Together,we promote unity,progress,mentorship,and share responsibility among all the pompomari alumni. Once again, Wellcome to the PFSA - where pompomari lives on through us.
